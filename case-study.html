01 — THE BRIEF

FROM CLIENT

"A boardroom that signals seriousness without feeling sterile. Hospitality-grade finishes, quiet acoustics, and a clear story for every visiting investor."

The client is a FinTech firm based in Marina One, seeking to consolidate two meeting rooms into a single flagship boardroom ahead of a Series C fundraise. The brief called for materials that read as high-end hospitality rather than corporate vanilla — with the acoustic performance to support both investor presentations and confidential calls.

Our response centred on a travertine-and-walnut material palette, linen acoustic panelling calibrated to 42dB isolation, and a custom bronzed steel credenza running the full length of the south wall — providing both storage and a visual anchor for the room.
